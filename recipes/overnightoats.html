Healthy overnight oats

Description

A perfect meal for a meal prep the night before! This high protein low fat breakfast is perfect for anyone on the go!

Ingrediants

  • Shaker bottle
  • 1/2 cup oatmeal
  • 1 cupalmond milk
  • 1/4 cup frozen berries
  • 1 serving your favorite greek yogurt

Instructions

  1. Add oats to shaker cup
  2. Pour oatmeal
  3. Add other Ingrediants
  4. shake bottle and put in the fridge over night

Wake up and take on the go!

Macronutrients

  • Calories - 289
  • Protein - 20
  • Carbs - 41
  • Fat - 6
