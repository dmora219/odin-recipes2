Protein Smoothie

Description

This will be a quick post workout smoothie! This post workout smoothie is filled with antioxidants,protein, and low in calories.

Ingrediants

  • 1 scoop of rule 1protein
  • 1 cup of frozen mixed berries
  • 2 cups of almond milk

Instructions

  1. Add almond milk into blender
  2. Add frozen berries
  3. Add protein powder
  4. Blend until fully blended

Enjoy!


Macronutrients

  • Calories - 256
  • Protein - 28
  • Carbs - 21
  • Fat - 7
