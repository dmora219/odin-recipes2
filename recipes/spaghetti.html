My Favorite Healthy Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This is going to be a healthy alternative to regular spaghetti dish. This low calorie, high protein dish is so good it will make you want to slap your momma!

Ingredients

  • 3 oz barilla protein spaghetti
  • 2 oz 93/7 ground beef
  • 2 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp salt and black pepper
  • (optional) for spice 2 tbsp crushed peppers

Instructions

  1. Put ground beef in a pan to cook
  2. boil spaghetti while beef is cooking
  3. add marinara sauce into ground beef
  4. add seasonings and crushed peppers while stiring
  5. add cream cheese, stir til it becomes creamy
  6. drain spaghetti add sauce

Enjoy! p.s. dont slap your momma!


Macronutrients

  • Calories - 770
  • Protein - 45
  • Carbs - 85
  • Fat - 22
